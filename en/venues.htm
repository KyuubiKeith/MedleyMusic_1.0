TRANSPORT

TO LEGENDOS CLUB

Bus: 10, 30, 34, 49, 53, 69, 1G, 103N

Trolleybus: 6, 10

Stop: "BRONIAUS LAURINAVICIAUS SKVERAS"

You can reach “Legendos” Club (former New York Club) from the city center by trolleybus 6 or buses 53 and 1G,which go to Žirmunai direction (get out at the bus stop “Broniaus Laurinaviciaus Skveras”). You can also arrive at the club from almost anywhere by buses 10, 30, 34, 49, 69 or trolleybus 10. Check more here!

TO TAMSTA CLUB

Bus: 11, 13, 31, 34, 74, 88

NIGHT BUS: 102N, 88N

Stop: "AUŠROS VARTAI"

You can reach “Tamsta Club” by buses 11, 13, 31, 34, 74, 88 at the bus stop “Aušros vartai” or by

trolleybuses 1, 2, 7, 20 and bus 53, which go to “Stotis” direction, getting out at the bus stop “Gėlių” and walking few hundred meters towards “Aušros Vartai”. Check more here:  http://stops.lt/vilnius/#stop/0440/map

http://stops.lt/vilnius/#stop/0417/map

http://stops.lt/vilnius/#stop/0412/map

TO LINDYHOP.LT CLUB

Bus: 1, 2, 3, 4, 12, 13, 16, 19, 23, 31, 34, 31, 42, 53, 54, 61, 74, 78, 82, 102N, 103N

Trolleybus: 1, 2, 7, 15, 16, 20

Stop: "STOTIS"

That’s an easy one! Just take any bus or trolleybus, that goes to “STOTIS” (“STATION”). Check it out here.

TO LINDY TRIP

Bus: 11, 53, 88, 1G

Trolleybus: 2, 3, 6, 12, 14, 20

Stop: "VINCO KUDIRKOS AIKŠTĖ"

It’s in the city center, so plenty of public transportation go there. Exit the bus or trolleybus at “Vinco Kudirkos aikštė” and walk ~10 m towards Gediminas ave.

TO VAIDILOS THEATER

Bus: 22, 73, 3G, 102N

TROLLEYBUS: 4, 10, 17

STOP: "JUOZO TUMO - VAIŽGANTO"

The closes stops are “Vinco Kudirkos aikštė” (read more how to get there at “Lindy trip” section) and “Juozo Tumo - Vaižganto”, both are in the city center, so many busses and trolleybusses go there. From “Vinco Kudirkos aikštė” walk towards Gediminas av., turn right, walk towards “Jakšto g. and then turn right again. From “Juozo Tumo - Vaižganto” stop walk towards Lukiškių square, Gediminas av. and turn left at Jakšto st.
